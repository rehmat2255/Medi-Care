About Dr. Maira Chughtai

With over 35 years of experience in general medicine, Dr. Maira Chughtai provides comprehensive healthcare services to patients of all ages. Her patient-centered approach focuses on preventive care and evidence-based treatment.

Dr. Chughtai completed her medical degree from King Edward Medical University and has been practicing since 1988. She has special interests in chronic disease management, women's health, and geriatric care.

Now offering convenient virtual consultations through Zoom, Dr. Chughtai brings her expertise directly to your home.

Services Offered:

  • General health consultations
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, etc.)
  • Preventive health screenings
  • Medication management
  • Second opinions
